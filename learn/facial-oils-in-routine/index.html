Mastering the Ritual:
Oil Application

Oils are finishing touches, sensorial anchors, and protective seals. Here is the correct order of application for maximum efficacy.

Reading time: 3 mins • Guide No. 03
Feature Image: Dropper Texture

The rule of thumb in skincare is texture: thin to thick. Because Sevioraè blends are lipid-rich, they generally belong at the end of your routine to seal in hydration. However, the nuance lies in whether you are waking up or winding down.

The Daily Rhythm

☀

Morning (AM)

The Goal: Glow & Protection.

  • 1. Gentle Cleanse
  • 2. Water-based Serums
  • 3. Sevioraè Blend (Use sparingly—1 to 2 drops pressed into damp skin).
  • 4. Sunscreen (Wait 2 mins for oil to absorb first).
☾

Evening (PM)

The Goal: Repair & Massage.

  • 1. Double Cleanse
  • 2. Treatment / Actives
  • 3. Moisturizer
  • 4. Sevioraè Blend (Generous layer to lock everything in).

Protocol: Mixing with Actives

Many users ask about Retinol, BHAs, and Acids. Oils are excellent companions to these strong actives because they support the lipid barrier that actives can sometimes strip away.

The Sandwich Method:
If your skin is sensitive, apply a thin layer of oil before your retinol to act as a buffer. If your skin is resilient, apply the retinol first, let it dry completely (5-10 mins), and press the oil on top to seal it.

Note: Never mix oil directly into a sunscreen bottle, as it can destabilize the SPF filters. Layer them separately.
