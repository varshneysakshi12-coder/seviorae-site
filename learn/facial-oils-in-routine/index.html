Guide: Facial oils in your routine

Oils are finishing touches, sensorial anchors and sometimes the star of your ritual. Here’s how to place them thoughtfully.

The guidance below is cosmetic in nature and based on how we expect the SevioraAc blends to behave on skin.

Layering basics

  • Apply facial oils after water-based serums and before or after moisturizer depending on how dry you feel.
  • For AM routines, let oil absorb for a few minutes before SPF to avoid pilling.
  • In PM routines, mix a drop into moisturizer if you prefer a lighter finish.

Pairing with actives

If you’re using exfoliating acids, retinoids or dermat-prescribed treatments, stagger application so oils don’t dilute them. Apply actives first, wait until fully dry, then gently press oil over the top. On buffer nights, layer oil before your active to cushion skin.

When in doubt, consult your dermatologist—it’s their call how often you can pair oils with medical treatments.

Choosing the right SevioraAc blend

Match your oil to the main concern you’re nurturing: breakout support (Acne-Prone Skin Blend), tone and luminosity (Dark Spot & Pigmentation Blend), shine control (Oily Skin & Pore Balance Blend), barrier comfort (Skin Barrier Repair Blend) or visible aging support (Anti-Aging Blend).

Read the Science page for the research pillars behind each blend, then keep a simple usage diary so you can track how your skin responds over time.
