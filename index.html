How Sevioraè© blends are developed

Each blend is guided by peer-reviewed literature on essential oils and plant oils in skin care, combined with a focus on texture, aroma and everyday ritual.

Concern-first thinking

Essential oils & plant oils

Essential oils and plant oils with relevant properties are then shortlisted, with attention to published data on irritation potential, barrier benefits or cosmetic effects. The goal is a blend that feels luxurious while remaining considered.

Iterative formulation

Texture, aroma, and how easily a blend layers into a routine all matter. As Sevioraè© grows, the research base and testing will expand further, including planned clinical and practitioner input.

Safety, patch testing & medical care

  • Always patch test on a small area (for example, the inner forearm) before first use.
  • Do not use on broken, actively inflamed or infected skin. Stop use and seek medical advice if irritation persists.
  • Sevioraè© products are cosmetics, not medicines. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease.
  • For diagnosed skin conditions such as eczema, psoriasis or severe acne, work with a dermatologist.

The Sevioraè© ritual

Facial oils work best when they sit inside a simple, consistent routine. Sevioraè© is being shaped to complement, not complicate, what you already do.

Morning (AM)

A gentle cleanse, hydrating layers and sun protection remain essential. Selected Sevioraè© blends are being designed to fit as a thin layer after water-based steps and before, or instead of, a final cream where appropriate.

Evening (PM)

Night routines are where richer textures and more deliberate massage make sense. PM-focused blends will be suited to slower application, breathing and touch, while still respecting skin sensitivity.

Layering with actives

As actives such as exfoliating acids or retinoids are introduced, oil blends need to be selected and used with care. Sevioraè© will share simple guidance on pairing blends with more active steps as the range launches.
