Privacy Policy

This policy explains how SevioraAc (Namah Group Product & Services (OPC) Pvt Ltd) expects to handle personal data during Phase-0.

We respect your privacy. The information below describes the data we collect when you interact with seviorae.com, why we collect it, and how you can reach us for questions.

Data we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, message contents) via forms or direct email.
  • Waitlist information (email address, concern preferences) so we can send launch updates.
  • Basic analytics such as page views or device type via privacy-friendly tools to improve the site.

How we use the data

  • Respond to your questions or requests.
  • Send waitlist emails or service updates (with an unsubscribe link in every message).
  • Understand which pages or guides are most helpful so we can improve them.

How we store and share information

  • Contact submissions are stored in secure email inboxes and cloud documents with restricted access.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • Trusted service providers (email platforms, analytics tools, payment gateways once active) will receive data only to perform services on our behalf and must protect it per their contracts.

Cookies & tracking

The site currently uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. If we add additional analytics or marketing cookies, we will update this policy and provide on-site notices.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from waitlist or marketing emails at any time using the link in the email.
  • Email care@seviorae.com to request access, correction or deletion of your contact data.
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings if desired.

Policy updates & contact

We may revise this policy as we move from Phase-0 to full launch. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Questions? Email care@seviorae.com.
