Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025 Namah Group Product & Services (OPC) Pvt Ltd

We respect your privacy. This document outlines how Sevioraè collects, uses, and protects your personal data. We believe in radical transparency—not just in our ingredients, but in our digital practices.

01. Data We Collect

To provide our services, we may collect the following:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, or similar identifiers provided via forms.
  • Contact Data: Email address and message contents sent via our contact forms.
  • Preference Data: Skin concerns or waitlist preferences you voluntarily submit.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics (page views, device type) to help us improve site performance.

02. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • To send waitlist updates or newsletters (only if you opted in).
  • To analyze website traffic patterns and improve user experience.

03. Storage & Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Data is stored in secure, restricted-access environments. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., email platforms, hosting services) only as necessary to perform services on our behalf. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

04. Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies for essential functionality and basic analytics. You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though this may affect how the website functions.

05. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.
  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data by emailing care@seviorae.com.
