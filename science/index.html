Methodology

Evidence, Not Hype.

We bridge the gap between ancient aromatherapy wisdom and modern cosmetic science. Every blend begins with a question, not a trend.

The Formulation Loop

A rigorous cycle of research, testing, and refinement.

01

Concern First

We map the everyday realities of specific skin issues—tightness, inflammation, or shine. We define the texture goal before selecting a single drop of oil.

02

Literature Review

Ingredients are screened through journals and safety databases. We review lipid profiles, comedogenic ratings, and sensitization risks.

03

Ritual Testing

A blend must perform in the real world. We test glide, absorption speed, and compatibility with SPF and active serums like retinol.

The Safety Standard

We formulate conservatively. Essential oils are powerful, so we treat them with respect.

  • Dilution: Essential oils are kept at cosmetic levels (often <1.5%) to minimize sensitization risk.
  • Patch Testing: We advocate for a 24-hour patch test behind the ear before full facial application.
  • Transparency: We list potential allergens naturally occurring in essential oils (like Limonene, Linalool).

The Future of Our Lab

Our Standard of Excellence is just the beginning. Rigourous independent lab testing for stability and microbial safety is next, followed by recruiting a dermatologist advisory panel.
