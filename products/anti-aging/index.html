Anti-Aging Blend

A velvety oil that wraps skin in omega-rich lipids and indulgent aromatics so nightly massage doubles as mindfulness. Think cushion, luminosity and a calm finish rather than heavy residue.

Crafted for people seeing dryness lines, dullness or a desire for slow evening rituals.

What to expect

A glide suited to gua sha or hand massage, gentle floral-citrus aromatics, and a finish that looks radiant rather than oily. Essential oils remain under 1% for comfort.

Always pair with SPF and professional advice for long-term photodamage care.

What this blend is designed to support

  • Helps skin feel cushioned and supple through omega-rich carrier oils.
  • Supports the look of elasticity by encouraging slow massage each night.
  • Softens the appearance of dryness lines with an emollient but breathable finish.

Key components & rationale

  • Carrier foundation: camellia seed, plum kernel and meadowfoam oils for silky glide.
  • Botanical concentrates: rosehip, pomegranate and babchi seed oil (a natural source of bakuchiol) to support luminosity.
  • Aromatic microdoses: neroli, rose otto and sandalwood to signal wind-down without overwhelming skin.

How this is expected to fit into a routine

Morning

Blend a single drop into moisturizer when skin feels tight, letting it absorb fully before sunscreen.

Evening

Warm 3–4 drops between fingertips and massage over damp skin after serums to lock in hydration.

Layering with actives

Alternate with retinoid nights or buffer potent actives by applying the oil first to reduce sensation, as directed by your professional.

Safety notes

  • Patch test before first use and pause if any tingling persists.
  • Do not use on compromised or freshly exfoliated skin.
  • Consult a dermatologist if you are pregnant, nursing or using prescription topicals.
  • Keep away from the eye contour and store away from direct heat.
