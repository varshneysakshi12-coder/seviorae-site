Formulation No. 05
In Lab Testing Series: Vitality

Anti-Aging Blend

A velvety oil that wraps skin in omega-rich lipids. Designed to support elasticity and turn nightly massage into mindfulness.

Availability Launching 2025

The Philosophy: We believe "anti-aging" is really about "pro-resilience." This blend focuses on cushioning the barrier with Omega 3, 6, and 9 fatty acids while using Babchi Seed (a gentle Bakuchiol source) to support cell turnover without the harshness of traditional retinoids.

Scent Profile Neroli, Sandalwood, Rose
Texture Velvety & Cushioned
Finish Radiant / Dewy

The Formulation

  • Base: Camellia Seed, Plum Kernel, Meadowfoam (for glide).
  • Botanicals: Rosehip, Pomegranate, Babchi Seed (Bakuchiol source).
  • Aromatics: Micro-doses of Neroli and Sandalwood for calming.

Full INCI list will be published upon launch.

The Ritual

AM: Mix 1 drop into moisturizer for a glow boost.
PM: Warm 3–4 drops and massage upwards. Excellent for Gua Sha.

Read the full ritual guide →

Safety & Contraindications

  • Patch test before first use. Discontinue if redness occurs.
  • Consult a doctor if pregnant or nursing (due to essential oils).
  • Store away from direct heat to preserve the cold-pressed lipids.
