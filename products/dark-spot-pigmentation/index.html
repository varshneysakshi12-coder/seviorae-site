Dark Spot & Pigmentation Blend

A cushiony blend that pairs antioxidant-dense plant oils with calming essential oils to support an even-looking glow. Built to accompany diligent SPF and dermatologist-directed care.

Ideal for nightly massage after brightening serums, especially on cheeks, forehead and other areas prone to lingering spots.

What to expect

A sunrise-toned oil thanks to sea buckthorn and rosehip extracts, plus a soft resinous aroma from frankincense. The finish stays moisturizing without feeling greasy.

Keep expectations cosmetic; stubborn pigment needs professional supervision.

What this blend is designed to support

  • Helps skin look more luminous with regular nightly massage.
  • Supports the appearance of even tone as spots fade naturally.
  • Offers a calming topcoat when brightening actives feel drying.

Key components & rationale

  • Carrier blend: rosehip, sea buckthorn, squalane and kukui nut for antioxidant support.
  • Essential oils: frankincense, helichrysum italicum and carrot seed for their traditional use in brightening rituals.
  • Botanical extracts: licorice root CO2 and turmeric fraction to complement gentle exfoliation plans.

How this is expected to fit into a routine

Morning

Use sparingly after moisturizer and before sunscreen if you enjoy a dewy finish; otherwise reserve for PM.

Evening

Apply 3–4 drops after water-based brightening serums, pressing over areas with visible spots.

Layering with actives

Alternate with dermatologist-prescribed treatments (such as hydroquinone or retinoids) or apply after they set so the oil seals hydration without interfering.

Safety notes

  • Patch test before adding to routines that already include acids or retinoids.
  • Pause use if skin feels hot or looks inflamed, and seek professional guidance.
  • Keep sunscreens company—use daily SPF 30+ and reapply during daylight exposure.
  • Store the bottle away from direct sunlight to protect the natural pigments.
