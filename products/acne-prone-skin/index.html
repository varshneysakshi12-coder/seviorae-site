Signature Blend No. 01
Join Waitlist Series: Clarity

Acne-Prone Skin Blend

A breathable, clarifying lipid layer designed to balance congestion without stripping the barrier.

Availability Coming Soon

The Philosophy: Breakouts often signal a barrier in distress, not just "dirty" skin. This blend uses Jojoba and Black Cumin Seed to mimic healthy sebum, signaling your skin to stop overproducing oil, while Tea Tree and Blue Tansy (diluted <1.5%) provide gentle clarity.

Scent Profile Herbaceous, Crisp, Green
Texture Dry Oil (Fast Absorption)
Finish Satin / Matte

The Formulation

  • Base: Jojoba Esters, Grapeseed Oil, Black Cumin Seed.
  • Actives: Tea Tree (Chemotype), Rosemary Verbenone, Lavender.
  • Support: Tamanu Oil for deep barrier buffering.

Full INCI list will be published upon launch.

The Ritual

AM: Press 1-2 drops into damp skin before SPF.
PM: Massage 3-4 drops as a final step to seal hydration.

Read the full ritual guide →

Safety & Contraindications

  • Essential oils are diluted for safety, but always patch test on the inner forearm.
  • Do not apply to open wounds or freshly picked blemishes.
  • If using prescription retinoids, consult your dermatologist before layering.
