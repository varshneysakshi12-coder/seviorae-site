Acne-Prone Skin Blend

Lightweight plant oils and carefully diluted essential oils that support skin prone to breakouts without stripping it. Built for people who want clarity plus a calm, breathable finish.

What to expect

A satin finish that sinks in quickly, making it easy to wear beneath sunscreen or makeup. Essential oil dilution is held under 1.5% to stay gentle while still offering the sensory lift acne-focused blends often miss.

Dermatologist care, prescription treatments and consistent sun protection remain the foundation for acne management.

What this blend is designed to support

  • Helps skin feel clearer and less congested through a light, slip-friendly oil film.
  • Encourages a balanced-looking complexion so SPF and makeup sit comfortably for longer.
  • Pairs antioxidant oils with comforting lipids to care for the look of post-blemish marks.

Key components & rationale

  • Carrier base of jojoba esters, grape seed and black cumin seed for breathable hydration.
  • Essential oil synergy (tea tree chemotype, rosemary verbenone, lavender) at cosmetic dilutions for clarity-supporting aromas.
  • Tamanu, hemp seed and vitamin-rich oils buffer the barrier when using clinical treatments.

How this is expected to fit into a routine

Morning

Press 2–3 drops over damp skin after hydrating serums and before sunscreen for a satin finish.

Evening

Massage a pea-sized amount over the face and neck as the final step after water-based products.

Layering with actives

Alternate nights when using exfoliating acids or prescription treatments, or apply the blend after the active has fully dried to cushion potential dryness.

Safety notes

  • Patch test on the inner forearm or behind the ear before applying to the face.
  • Do not apply on open blemishes or freshly picked skin.
  • Discontinue use if persistent redness or heat develops and consult a dermatologist.
  • Keep away from the eye area and store in a cool, dark space.
