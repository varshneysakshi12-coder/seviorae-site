Oily Skin & Pore Balance Blend

What to expect

A weightless oil-serum hybrid with a herbal-woody aroma and a soft-focus finish. No heavy silicones, just fast-slip carriers and pore-hugging botanicals.

Dermatologist guidance still matters for persistent oil production or acne.

What this blend is designed to support

  • Helps reduce the look of midday shine so skin appears more balanced.
  • Softens the appearance of enlarged pores by smoothing texture.
  • Keeps makeup and SPF layers feeling comfortable by preventing dehydration.

Key components & rationale

  • Base: squalane, hazelnut and grape seed oils for featherlight slip.
  • Essential oils: clary sage, cedarwood atlas and cypress for their balancing aromatherapy profiles.
  • Plant complexes: green tea and niacinamide-rich ferments to keep the finish fresh-looking.

How this is expected to fit into a routine

Morning

Mix a single drop with moisturizer or press directly onto the T-zone before sunscreen.

Evening

Use 2–3 drops after watery serums for slip during facial massage or gua sha.

Layering with actives

Alternate or buffer exfoliating acids by applying this blend afterwards to maintain comfort.

Safety notes

  • Patch test first, especially if you are already using chemical exfoliants.
  • Pause use on freshly shaved areas or active breakouts.
  • Keep out of eyes and store upright with the cap tightly closed.
