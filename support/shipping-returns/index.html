Shipping & returns

This page outlines the intended policy for Phase-0. Final wording will be updated before orders open.

Sevioraè is operated by Namah Group Product & Services (OPC) Pvt Ltd. We currently plan to ship only within India from our Bangalore fulfilment partner.

Shipping plan (from launch)

  • Serviceable region: All courier-accessible pin codes within India.
  • Dispatch window: 3–5 working days after order confirmation while Phase-0 batches are small.
  • Delivery partners: Reputed courier services with doorstep tracking. Tracking details will be emailed as soon as parcels leave our studio.
  • Shipping fees: Introductory flat fee (to be announced) with free shipping above a minimum order value.
  • Cash on delivery: Not available at launch. UPI and card payments will be routed through a secure gateway.

Returns & replacements (from launch)

  • Due to the nature of skincare, we cannot accept returns of opened or used products.
  • If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact care@seviorae.com within 48 hours of delivery with photos and your order ID.
  • We will arrange a replacement or refund for verified transit damage or packing errors.
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 working days after approval.

Future improvements

  • Expanding shipping to select international markets once stability testing in new climates is complete.
  • Faster dispatch times as batch sizes grow and inventory is stocked in multiple hubs.
  • Pick-up for recycling empty bottles as part of a sustainability pilot.

Questions? Visit the Support page or write to care@seviorae.com.
