Service Standards

Region: India Only Status: Pre-Launch Policy

We believe the ritual begins the moment the parcel leaves our studio. Below are the operational standards for our upcoming launch phase.

01. Logistics & Shipping

  • 📍
    Region

    We actively service all courier-accessible pin codes within India via our Bangalore fulfillment partner.

  • ⏱
    Timeline

    Dispatch occurs within 3–5 working days. Tracking is emailed immediately upon handover.

  • 📦
    Fees

    Flat-rate introductory shipping. Complimentary shipping is applied to orders above the threshold (TBA).

02. Returns & Damages

  • ↺
    The Hygiene Standard

    Due to the nature of cosmetic products, we cannot accept returns of opened, used, or unsealed bottles.

  • ⚠
    Transit Damage

    If your glass bottle arrives broken, email care@seviorae.com within 48 hours with photos. We will expedite a replacement.

  • 💳
    Refunds

    Approved refunds are processed to the original payment source (UPI/Card) within 7–10 working days.

03. The Future Roadmap

As we move from Phase-0 to full scale, we are working on:

  • International shipping to select markets (pending stability testing).
  • Next-day dispatch for metro cities.
  • A "Bottle Back" recycling program for empty glass units.
