Assistance

How can we help?

Common questions regarding our launch timeline, formulation philosophy, and safety protocols.

What is Sevioraè? +

Sevioraè is a concern-first facial oil brand created by Sakshi Varshney under Namah Group Product & Services (OPC) Pvt Ltd. Each blend is cosmetic and focuses on ritual, texture, and evidence-informed ingredient pairings.

When will the blends launch? +

We are currently in Phase-0: stability, safety, and sensory testing. The founding five blends will launch in small batches once packaging and safety documentation are finalized. Join the waitlist on the home page to be notified first.

Where do you ship? +

Launch shipments will be limited to addresses within India. International fulfilment is part of our long-term roadmap but is not confirmed for Phase-0.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We expect to offer UPI, major debit/credit cards, and select wallet options through a secure payment gateway. Cash on delivery (COD) will not be available at launch.

Are the products medical treatments? +

No. Sevioraè blends are cosmetic facial oils. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always work with a dermatologist for diagnosed conditions or prescription routines.

Should I patch test? +

Yes—patch testing on the inner forearm for 24 hours is mandatory for every new blend, especially when you already use actives like AHAs, BHAs, or retinoids.

Where can I learn about ingredients? +

The Science page and Learn hub summarize the research we lean on. We use plain language and avoid medical claims so you can pair the information with professional advice.

Still have questions?

Our concierge team is available for product guidance or press inquiries.
