Support & FAQ

Phase-0 Sevioraè is a research-informed preview. The answers below explain what to expect during this soft launch period.

Need personalised help? Email care@seviorae.com or send a note through the contact form.

Frequently asked questions

  • What is Sevioraè?

    Sevioraè is a concern-first facial oil brand created by Sakshi Varshney under Namah Group Product & Services (OPC) Pvt Ltd. Each blend is cosmetic and focuses on ritual, texture and evidence-informed ingredient pairings.

  • When will the blends launch?

    We are in Phase-0: stability, safety and sensory testing. The founding five blends will launch in small batches once packaging, safety documentation and education materials are ready. Join the waitlist on the home page to receive updates.

  • Where do you ship?

    Launch shipments will be limited to addresses within India. International fulfilment is part of the roadmap but not confirmed for Phase-0.

  • What payment methods will you support?

    We expect to offer UPI, major debit/credit cards and select wallet options through a secure payment gateway. Cash on delivery (COD) will not be available at launch.

  • Are the products medical treatments?

    No. Sevioraè blends are cosmetic facial oils. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease. Always work with a dermatologist for diagnosed conditions or prescription routines.

  • Should I patch test?

    Yes—patch testing on the inner forearm for 24 hours is mandatory for every new blend, especially when you already use actives like AHAs, BHAs or retinoids.

  • How do I join the waitlist?

    Submit the email form on the home page or write to care@seviorae.com with the subject “Waitlist”. You can unsubscribe any time via the link in every update.

  • Where can I learn about ingredients?

    The Science page and Learn hub summarise the research we lean on. We use plain language and avoid medical claims so you can pair the information with professional advice.

Need more detail?

Read the Shipping & Returns plan, explore blend research on the Science page, or contact us directly.
