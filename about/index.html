Sakshi Varshney, Founder

A Note from the Founder

"I didn't start Sevioraè because I wanted to launch a brand. I started it because I was tired of guessing."

My journey into skincare began like many others—confused by trends and overwhelmed by product shelves. But as a lifelong learner, I couldn't settle for marketing fluff. I needed to understand the why.

I spent years diving into peer-reviewed dermatological journals, studying the chemical profiles of botanical oils, and understanding how they interact with the skin barrier. I realized that while modern science gives us the data, ancient tradition gives us the ritual.

Sevioraè is the result of that intersection. Working with Namah Group, we are building a sanctuary where safety, science, and sensory luxury coexist.

Sakshi Varshney Founder, Sevioraè

Our Philosophy

We believe that skincare should not be a battle against your face, but a supportive dialogue. We reject the "burn it off" mentality of harsh actives in favor of "feed and repair." Every drop of Sevioraè is formulated to respect the skin's microbiome and lipid barrier.
